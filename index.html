Who am I?

I am a passionate individual who loves work that brings meaning to people's lives. As an creative whose life intersects with technology, I represent STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) and use that experience to uplift marginalized communities through increased access to art and technology. My heart is at the intersection of humanity and technology. I want to use computers and technology to help people and make life more accessible. Access, design, creation, opportunity. A lifelong learner, I always seek ways to improve upon my existing skills while endlessly acquiring new ones - continual improvement is a lifestyle for me. My goal is simple: I want to leave the world a better place than I found it.
