DESIGN

I've always had a creative eye and a deep sense of empathy. This has led to me gaining experience in photography, illustration, and graphic design. I've also always had to pick up new skills out of necessity; I needed to get things done, and I didn't have time to outsource everything that was required!

As I learned about software development, I noticed that I had a natural proclivity for front-end work and visual design. Call it intuition, or call it a decade+ experience of staring at screens, but I love working with the parts of a product that a user will interact with/experience directly.

I am also a big fan of the Aesthetic-Usability Effect. I believe that creating and enjoying beautiful things are a critical part of the human experience.
